MedEye

A visual recognition app that helps users to understand the pharmaceuticals market better. The goal of this is to reduce information asymmetry by allowing users to instantly gather data on a specific type of drug.

Simple & Clean

Every medication has benefits and risks with multitude of side effects, but there is a big gap between what customers know and what customers buy and take.

FAQ

Your questions, answered here

Why make this app?

We realized that there was a huge gap in what people know about medicine and what they take. This gap gave us the oppourtunity to make this app and we truly believe that this will help sustain the health of many people.

Who are we targeting?

Our target market is for anyone who gets sick and has an mobile device in a first world country where there are pharmaceutical stores and resources for obtaining medicine.

Is this app free?

This app is completely free. We don't charge for sustainable health!

What APIs did you use and how?

We used mobile text reading and medicine information APIs provided by Google Cloud Services.

What data sources did you pull from?

We puled data sources from all around the web to find statistics on medicine buying error and death rates.

What are your differences from similar apps?

We provide users a way to pick the right medication in a store front setting, whereas most other apps try to create personalized medication.
